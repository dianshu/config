# Chinese Tech Platforms Strategy

## Platforms and SearXNG Queries

### Zhihu (知乎)
- Query: `site:zhihu.com {topic}`
- Focus: Q&A format, professional discussions
- Good for: Technical explanations, comparisons, user experiences

### Juejin (掘金)
- Query: `site:juejin.cn {topic}`
- Focus: Developer articles, tutorials, technical deep dives
- Good for: Implementation guides, best practices, code examples

### SegmentFault (思否)
- Query: `site:segmentfault.com {topic}`
- Focus: Technical Q&A, problem-solving
- Good for: Troubleshooting, specific technical issues

### CSDN Blog
- Query: `site:blog.csdn.net {topic}`
- Focus: Technical blogs, tutorials
- Good for: Step-by-step guides, technical explanations

### OSChina (开源中国)
- Query: `site:oschina.net {topic}`
- Focus: Open source projects, tech news
- Good for: Project discovery, Chinese open source alternatives

### V2EX
- Query: `site:v2ex.com {topic}`
- Focus: Developer community, discussions
- Good for: Community opinions, tool recommendations

## When to Activate Chinese Tech Module
- Topic contains Chinese characters
- Researching Chinese products (Baidu, Alibaba, Tencent tools)
- Looking for Chinese market perspectives
- User specifically requests Chinese sources
- Popular Chinese alternatives to Western tools

## Search Tips
- Use both Chinese and English terms
- Include brand names in Chinese (e.g., "阿里云" for Alibaba Cloud)
- Try simplified characters
- Search for specific product names (e.g., "微信小程序" for WeChat Mini Programs)

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Zhihu
- Look for answers with high upvotes
- Check author credentials and expertise
- Focus on longer, detailed responses

### Juejin
- Prioritize articles with good engagement metrics
- Look for official technology company accounts
- Check publication dates for currency

### V2EX
- Community discussions provide honest opinions
- Good for finding lesser-known tools and alternatives
- Check thread engagement for popular topics
